About Us

Collect plastic from daily use and turn them into interesting. We strive for sustainability and the art of reusable sources.

You can make a difference by submitting your artwork either digitally or physically. The exhibition will be on May 29th, in various Brooklyn Parks, and the Brooklyn Museum as the main exhibition

Anyone can be an artist! We accpet all kinds of artwork and the winner of the competition gets a chance to showcase their project in the Brooklyn Museum, a page on the magezine, and a limited edition T-shirt.
